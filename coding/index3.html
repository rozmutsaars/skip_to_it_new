why skip to it?

skip to it is based on the successful Nette Toilette initiative that is popular in Germany. By advertising public bathrooms that are available for all to use, we can create a greater sense of openness and community

accessible

we will always place accessibility at the heart of skip to it, publicising the availability of gender neutral, ground floor and accessible loos.


economical

statistics from Germany suggest that listing public loos saves money, and attracts customers to businesses!


welcoming

we really strongly believe in community. when you open your doors to the public, you are building community.
